Humidity

   Though temperature is the main talking point for this project, it's important to look at other factors that play into weather. Though it would be nice to live in a world where it's always 70 F, it wouldn't be so nice if it was always 100% humid. The sweet spot it seems is between 60 - 90% humidity where the majority of the random cities land. The most surprising takeaway is the fact that the driest records are not 20 degrees on either side of the equator, where the majority of the world's deserts are located. In this graph, no real trend is easily attainable. However, it does show the differance between the Northern and Southern hemispheres in terms of land mass.
