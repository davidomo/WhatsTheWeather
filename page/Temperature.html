Temperature

   As expected, temperature significantly rises as towns are approaching the equator. However, this does not mean the highest temperature was at the equator. From this random sample, the town with the highest recorded temperature (104.18 F) was Kabo from the Central African Republic. The city center lies 7.6949 degrees North of the equator. Conversely, the lowest temperature was not at the poles and furthest away from the equator. The lowest temperature recorded (-37.66 F) belong to the town of Batagay, Russia. Oddly enough, the town closest to either the North or South was not that cold at all. Barrow, Alaska was the furthest town recorded from the equator at 89.58339 degrees North. The temp? A balmy 36.95 F.
